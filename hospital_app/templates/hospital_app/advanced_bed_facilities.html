Advanced Bed Facilities

Our hospitals are equipped with state-of-the-art bed facilities, ensuring comfort and care for every patient. We offer a range of advanced bed options to cater to different medical needs.

Our Bed Facilities

ICU Beds

Our Intensive Care Unit (ICU) beds are equipped with advanced monitoring systems and life-support equipment to provide critical care to patients.

General Ward Beds

Our general ward beds offer a comfortable and safe environment for patients recovering from surgery or illness.

Private Room Beds

Our private room beds provide a quiet and private space for patients who require a more personalized care experience.

Maternity Beds

Our maternity beds are designed to provide comfort and support to expectant mothers during labor and delivery.

Pediatric Beds

Our pediatric beds are specially designed to cater to the needs of our youngest patients, ensuring their comfort and safety.
